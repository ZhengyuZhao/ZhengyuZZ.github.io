About Me

I am a PhD student at the Data Science Group, Radboud University, under the supervision of Prof. Martha Larson. My research interests include computer vision and multimedia. Much of my research experience has concentrated on scene recognition and image forensics. Recently, my work has focused increasingly on multimedia for humanity, such as disaster image retrieval from social media.

I obtained my MSc degree from Tianjin University, China in 2017, with the thesis on blind forensics of image filtering operations. Before that, I did my undergraduate study in communication engineering at Hainan University, China.
