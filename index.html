Welcome to Runmin Cong's Homepage

Hi！My name is Runmin Cong (丛润民), a third year Ph.D candidate at School of Electrical and Information Engineering, Tianjin University, P.R. China, supervised by Prof. Qingming Huang (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Jianjun Lei. From Dec. 2016 to Feb. 2017, I worked with Prof. Weisi Lin (IEEE/IET Fellow) as a visiting student at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. Since May 2018, I have been working with Head & Prof. Sam Kwong (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Junhui Hou as a Research Associate at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Kowloon, Hong Kong.
