About Me

I am a first-year PhD candidate in computer science at Radboud University, under the supervision of Prof. Martha Larson Before that, I obtained my Msc degree in information engineering from Tianjin University, China, in Jan. 2017.
My research interests include computer vision and multimedia understanding, and my experience has been about applications in scene recognition and image forensics.

Research Interests:

  • Computer Vision and Intelligent Video System
  • Saliency Detection for Image and Video
  • Remote Sensing Image Processing
  • Underwater Image Enhancement
