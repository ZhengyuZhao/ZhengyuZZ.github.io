About Me

I am a first-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at Radboud University , under the supervision of Prof. Martha Larson. Before that, I obtained my Msc degree from Tianjin University in 2017, with the thesis on blind forensics of image filtering operations.

My research focuses on multimedia understanding and analysis. Much of my research experience has concentrated on specific computer vision applications such as scene recognition and image forensics. Recently, I have been increasingly interested in multimedia techniques that have impact on human society, such as retrieval of flooding-related social images.
